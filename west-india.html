Explore West India

Konkan is a stunning coastal belt along the western coast of India, stretching through Maharashtra, Goa, and Karnataka. Known for its natural beauty, rich traditions, ancient temples, fresh seafood, and peaceful coastal lifestyle, it is one of the most scenic regions in the country.

Culture of Konkan

  • Traditional Konkani & Marathi customs
  • Grand celebration of Ganesh Chaturthi
  • Famous folk arts: Dashavatara, Koli Dance

Climate

  • Pleasant winters
  • Warm summers
  • Heavy monsoon with lush green scenery
