Explore East India

East India is a region of India that includes the states of Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha, and West Bengal. It is culturally rich, with historical sites, diverse ecosystems, and traditional cuisine influenced by rivers, forests, and coastal life.

Known for lush green landscapes, tea gardens, tribal heritage, classical dance forms, and ancient temples, East India offers a unique blend of nature and culture that attracts tourists from around the world.

Each state offers something special — Bengal’s literature and tea estates, Odisha’s temples, Bihar’s Buddhist history, and Jharkhand’s waterfalls and forests — making East India a region rich in beauty and heritage.
